Analyse de différentes intelligences artificielles

Report

Abstract:
Our project is an investigation on the nature of neural networks in the wider context of artificial intelligence and on its inner workings in image recognition technology. We described, analyzed and tested two methods we used to train neural networks specifically for numbers’ images recognition: back propagation and genetic algorithms. In our report you will find a detailed mathematical and conceptual description of what is artificial intelligence in general and what constitutes a neural network more specifically. While there are many more ways to train neural networks, we only explain in our report the mathematics and functioning behind back propagation and genetic algorithms. Since we have put into practice both training methods by programming our own neural networks with Python, the report includes our resultsfor the efficiency of both methods.
*French only

Strange House

Puzzle game - Python - Command Line

Strange House is a puzzle game developed with python. The game was designed for the command line. The game’s goal? To escape. To do so, the player needs to solve different puzzles presented as codes the player needs to find. All the clues are provided through the player’s interaction. With various commands, the player navigates through different rooms and interact with its environment to find clues and objects.

“You woke up in your house, but it seems a bit strange. Some things seem different then they were yesterday, others appeared, and some disappeared. Uneasy, you decided to get out, but the front door is locked. What will you do?”

*To run the game, python and matplotlib need to be installed.

miRide

HTML - CSS - JavaScript - Web design

For a class project, Unus, a team of five students, created a web page to showcase an imaginary product: miRide. The site's purpose was to convince future clients to download miRide through the page's design and -made up- arguments. Throughout the project, our team had to have great communication to coordinate the web page's development as well as great teamwork to help each other out, depending on everyone's strength and weaknesses.

"myRide is a mobile application perfect for anyone in Ottawa who travels without a car. It compares a variety of ride services based on various filters such as prices and traveling distances to fit everyone’s preferences."
